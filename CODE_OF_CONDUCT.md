# Code of Conduct

## Our standard

This project is a professional, welcoming space. Contributors and maintainers
are expected to:

- Be respectful, considerate, and constructive.
- Welcome newcomers and differing viewpoints.
- Give and accept feedback gracefully, focused on the work.
- Assume good faith.

The following are not tolerated:

- Harassment, insults, or discriminatory language or imagery.
- Personal or political attacks.
- Publishing others' private information without consent.
- Sustained disruption of discussion or review.

## Scope

This code applies in all project spaces — issues, pull requests, commits, and
discussions — and when an individual is representing the project in public.

## Enforcement

Report unacceptable behavior to **hello@plainsight-systems.com**. Reports are
handled confidentially. Maintainers may remove, edit, or reject contributions,
comments, and contributors that violate this code, and may escalate to a
temporary or permanent ban for serious or repeated violations.
